Our mission

At Eventy we have the goal of building a platform on mobile that will help people in creating events on the go. Our goal was to make it as easy as possible to create, add friends, and immediately share an event. Along with helping make it easy to create an event on the go, we strive to help connect people to events that interest them around them every day. From concerts to job fairs, everyone will find an event that interests them. Join us in bringing events to life and helping connect people through events!
